My name is Kai, and I'm an independent developer based in Seattle.

This is a little space on the internet where I showcase the apps that I've built and share my journey as an indie developer.

I started building my own apps because I loved the idea of making something that people use, and having complete creative freedom of my time and work. I published my first app in 2022, and have gone on to publish 4 more.

I do everything by myself - design, engineering and marketing. It is very satisfying work, and each app I build is a little bit better than the last one!

In my prior life, I was one of the first Product Managers at Duolingo. I was fortunate that my work took me many places (literally), and I even got to start Duolingo's first office outside the US!
